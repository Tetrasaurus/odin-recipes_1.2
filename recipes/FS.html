(FS) - Formation Skydiving

This will be the most important skill set to learn after becoming a qualifed skydiver. During the AFF course you learn how to fly your own body, safety protocols to look after yourself and the very basics of being a skydiver without too much information over load.

(FS) - is falling to the ground in the neutral(belly) position learnt in the AFF course. However this position is improved so that moving around the air becomes more efficient and natural to fly

Requirements

As this is your foundation to group flying there is very little needed
  • A-license (completed AFF course)

How to get your (FS) rating

  1. Find a (FS) coach
  2. Get brief from coach on improved flying position,safety flying with others and complete proficiency jumps
    • Linked exits
    • Flying position
    • Fast & slow fall
    • Side SLides
    • Turns
    • Swoop to pin(safe approaches)
    • Break-off heights
    • Tracking away from group

  3. Jump with three other persons and complete (x) amount of points to be awarded your (FS) rating, showing altitude awareness throughout and good break-off track
  4. Your now ready to jump with others! Its recommended to keep to small groups until you are comfortable flying with others and all the above becomes second nature!
